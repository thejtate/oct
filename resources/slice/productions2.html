“Doctor Doolittle”

July 27-31, 2011
Dramatized by Olga Fricker
Burg Theatre

Is a rather strange doctor who shuns human patients in favour of animals, with whom he can speak in their own languages. He lives in Puddleby-on-the-Marsh. He has a few human friends, Mathew Mugg for one. Join the Dr's animal family including Polynesia a parrot, Gub-Gub a pig, Chee-Chee a monkey and a Pushmi-Pullyu, as they take him on the adventure of a life time!
