“She Blinded Me with Science!”

It’s your Un-Birthday, and we should celebrate! There is a party just waiting for you to arrive. But not just you, a hundred of your friends should fill the tables nicely, and there will be no end of the muchness to be had. So, join me, The Mad Hatter, and celebrate your Un-Birthday at Mind Games, the newest exhibit at Science Museum Oklahoma. For certain, you can’t have an Un-Birthday Party without tea and nibbles…and cake! Chef Ryan Parrot will be offering up the finest first-class finger food hors d’oeuvres with teas fit for a queen, while Gigi’s Cupcakes of Oklahoma City delights us all with personal Un-Birthday cupcakes, all of this to musical styling of the Wunderjazz trio. So, it’s settled; you, me, and a hundred of your closest friends and family, all of us to have our Un-Birthday party at Mind Games in Science Museum Oklahoma on the un-birthdate of your choice.
