Spring 2011 Classes

Seasonal classes are a perfect way to allow your child to explore their creativity and continue to fine tune their performing telents during the school year. With a variety of classes throughout the week, for children aged 4-12 years old, we have something for every age group and experience level. Check out the class descriptions below, and don't forget to Contact Us if you have any more questions.

Spotlight Production Class

Saturdays, March 26-May 21 • 10am-12pm
7 years old and up • $200

Every young actor's chance to be a star! Students in this class will experience the entire, exciting play production process, from auditions and learning lines to dress rehearsals and performances. This class will culminate in an all kid-cast production, and there is a role for everyone in the play that we will develop. In this special program, students will learn theatre skills and gain acting experience through hands-on participation in the production process. There are also opportunities for technically inclined students who would like to be involved in behind-the-scenes aspects of theatre production. On with the show!

Homeschool Theatre Classes

Session II—Monologues
Mondays, April 4-April 25 • 10am-12pm
6 & up • $100

These classes will highlight the basics of creative drama and creative movement. Using stories, games, and simple crafts, children will learn to follow sequential directions, work together as a team, and use their imaginations to create characters as they play, move, pretend, and invent. At the end of class performance, family and friends are invited to see the children perform as the stars of the show! The content of this class varies, so children can join us multiple times. All participants must be potty trained in order to attend class.
