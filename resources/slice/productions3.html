Touring Productions

Our Current Touring Show Is:

“River Rat and Cat”

By Y York

No one has ever understood the mysterious friendship of River Rat and Cat. Cat is imaginative, grand, fussy and thoroughly needy, while Rat is material, practical and thoroughly self-reliant. The day River Rat saved Cat from drowning in the river, they forged a unlikely friendship. But, their downstream neighbor, Dale Beaver must break up this friendship. He needs the one remaining tree on the river bank, so his dam will remain the tallest on the river. But this tree is owned by Rat and home to Cat. Will Dale Beaver break up this friendship and convince Rat to sell him Cat's home?

The Oklahoma Arts Council can provide fee support to organizations or institutions outside the Oklahoma City area who wish to bring these shows into their communities. The money is on a first-come first serve basis! For more information call the Box Office at Oklahoma Children's Theatre at (405) 951-0011.


All you need is:

  • 25' X 25' performance space
  • Power source
  • Place to change clothes
  • Access to the space for loading and unloading
