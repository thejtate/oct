Each week campers will experience an exciting ocean adventure while they brush up their showmanship skills and learn performing arts basics in an atmosphere of creativity and imagination. Audiences will be captivated and mesmerized by the "sea-sational" camp performances in theatre, magic, dance, and more!

Camp Daily Activities

Campers will experience the magic of theatre, brush up their performance skills, and learn theatre basics in a fun environment of creativity and imagination. Each 5-day camp features theatre camps, specialty camps like magic or dance, and lots of fun with a different ocean adventure theme each week.

Camp hours are 9:00am to 4:00pm; Pre-School Morning Camps are 9:00am to 12:00pm.
Before & After Care is available 8am-9am & 4pm-6pm.

Campers spend each day with their teacher, learning concepts and exploring techniques in theatre, music, magic, dance, film, or other focus area while preparing for their end of the week performance.

A "Fun Time" class is included each day—fun activities in art, music, stage combat, improvisation, movement, puppetry, crafts, indoor and outdoor games, and much more!

Enjoy the best sack-lunch hour around with after lunch entertainment like Movie Madness, Magic Trick Demonstrations, Games on the Lawn, Story Time, Variety Show, and more!
