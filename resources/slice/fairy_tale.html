Oklahoma Children's Theatre provides exceptional opportunities for children to develop their creative potential through participation in theatrical productions and related interactive educational experiences. Each year, we produce eight main stage Productions plus a statewide tour to rural and underserved communities. We offer year-round youth acting classes, theatre camps and after-school programs.

Your Sponsorship provides the opportunity for many young Oklahomans and their families to experience professional, live children's theatre and helps them fulfill their creative dreams and desires. We look forward to your support of this magical and imaginative evening for families young and old. You are never too old to fall in love with Cinderella, King Arthur or a friendly dragon!
